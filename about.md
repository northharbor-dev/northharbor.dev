NorthHarbor Development is the open source home for principled tools and frameworks focused on human-AI collaboration. Our projects are developed in public, governed transparently, and designed to be useful to practitioners and researchers alike.

## What We Build

We focus on the intersection of human agency and AI capability — frameworks, specifications, and tools that help people work with AI responsibly.

Our flagship project, [HALOS](https://halos.northharbor.dev) (Human-Agent Living Operating System), is an open framework for principled collaboration between humans and intelligent agents. It defines core principles, governance processes, and machine-readable specifications that any team or tool can adopt.

## How We Work

- **Public by default.** Code, specifications, and governance discussions happen in the open on GitHub.
- **Principled.** Human primacy, attribution, transparency of AI involvement, and ethical guardrails are foundational to everything we ship.
- **Governed through process.** Changes to frameworks and specifications go through an RFC-style proposal process with public review.
